Inside Competitive Cheerleading

More than just cheers

In recent years, competitive cheerleading has grown in popularity. While this form of cheerleading is pretty different from typical sideline cheerleading, there still remains a stigma that competitive cheerleading should not be considered a sport. Coaches and athletes have been continuously been creating routines and showing off new and harder skills, in order to not only improve the sport itself, but to also be recognized as a sport. This webiste will take you through the what competitive cheerleading is, key elements and skills and a look at my story through competitive cheerleading.
